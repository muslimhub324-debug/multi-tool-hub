Understanding Your BMI

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. It's a useful tool for adults to get a general idea of their weight status.

BMI Ranges for Adults:

  • Below 18.5: Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9: Healthy Weight
  • 25.0 – 29.9: Overweight
  • 30.0 and Above: Obesity
